HardRush DJ Competition

As the years have gone by, we at HardRush have had difficulties keeping up with all the amazing up-and-coming DJs in and around Southern California. Looking to broaden our horizons and diversify our lineups, HardRush is holding a competition open to all SoCal HardDance DJs for a time slot at the next HardRush. This edition of HardRush will take place on August 19th, 2023 in LA.

To enter, please submit a 10-15 minute video of you DJing to the link below no later than 11:59 PM on June 9th. HardRush will review all submissions and choose our top 5 videos; each of the top 5 entrants will receive free admission to HardRush. The chosen videos will then be uploaded to our YouTube channel on July 1st. The DJ whose video receives the most likes on YouTube by 11:59 PM on July 31st will be given a time slot at HardRush, and it will not be an opening or closing slot.

Rules:

  • All entrants must be at least 18 years of age before the date of the event.
  • Contest is open only to individuals that have not played a previous HardRush event.
  • Only 1 video is allowed to be submitted per artist.
  • Video should be a recording of your live mixing; we are not interested in your video editing skills.
  • Videos greater than 15 minutes are automatically disqualified.
  • HardRush will choose the finalists based on mixing, track selection, and artist performance.
  • When submitting your video, please include your DJ name and best contact information.
  • Once a video is submitted, please email contest@hardrush.us your contact information including Name, DJ Name, photo, logo (if available), and how to contact you.
  • Artists are responsible for their own transportation to and from the event.
  • Videos must not contain nudity, substance use, or reference to any illegal activity.
  • Any speech, images, or lyrics that depict hate, slander, or defamation based on race, age, or sexual orientation is automatic disqualification from the contest.
  • Finalists found to be using or suspected of using bot or paid clicks will automatically be disqualified.
  • All video submissions become property of HardRush LLC to use as we see fit, including any future competitions.

Good luck to all participants, and we can't wait to see your amazing talent on display! Thank you for being a part of this exciting competition, and we look forward to seeing you at HardRush on August 19th, 2023.

For any questions or further information, please feel free to reach out to us at contest@hardrush.us.
