HardRush DJ Competition

As the years have gone by we have had difficulties keeping up with all the amazing up and coming DJs in and around Southern California. Looking to broaden our horizons and diversify our line ups, HardRush is having a competition open to all SoCal HardDance DJs for a time slot at the next HardRush. This edition of HardRush will take place on August 19th, 2023 in LA.

To enter, please submit a 10-15 minute video of you DJing to the link below no later than 11:59pm on June 9th. HardRush will review all submissions and choose our top 5 videos, each of the top 5 entrants will receive free admission to HardRush. The chosen videos will then be uploaded to our YouTube channel on July 1st.

The DJ whose video gets the most likes by 11:59pm July 31st will be given a non-opening time slot at HardRush.

Rules:

  • All entrants must be at least 18 years of age before the date of the event
  • Contest is open only to individuals that have not played a previous HardRush event.
  • Only 1 video is allowed to be submitted per artist
  • Video should be a recording of your live mixing, we are not interested in your video editing skills.
  • Videos greater than 15 minutes are automatically disqualified
  • Submitted video file name should be your DJ name only.
  • HardRush will choose the finalists based on mixing, track selection, and artist performance.
  • Once the video is submitted, please email hardrushcontest@gmail.com your contact information including Name, DJ Name, photo, logo (if available), and how to contact you.
  • Artist is responsible for their own transportation to and from the event.
  • Videos must not contain nudity, substance use, or reference to any illegal activity
  • Any speech, images, or lyrics that depict hate, slander, or defamation based on race, age, or sexual orientation is automatic disqualification from the contest.
  • Finalists found to be using or suspected of using bot or paid clicks will automatically be disqualified

Good luck to all participants, and we can't wait to see your amazing talent on display! Thank you for being a part of this exciting competition, and we look forward to seeing you at HardRush on August 19th, 2023.

For any questions or further information, please feel free to reach out to us at hardrushcontest@gmail.com.
